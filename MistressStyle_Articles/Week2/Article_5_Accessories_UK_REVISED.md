## Accessory Care & Maintenance

### Caring for Your Jewellery

First, store pieces separately to prevent scratching. Additionally, remove jewellery before showering or swimming. Moreover, clean with a jewellery cloth monthly. Finally, get professional cleaning annually.

### Maintaining Leather Goods

Condition leather 2-3 times per year. Furthermore, store items stuffed with tissue. Additionally, keep leather away from water and heat. Finally, get professional cleaning when needed.

### Protecting Your Shoes

Rotate shoes rather than wearing the same pair daily. Moreover, use shoe trees to maintain shape. Additionally, clean shoes after each wear. Most importantly, repair damage before it worsens.

### Preserving Your Sunglasses

Always store sunglasses in their case. Furthermore, clean lenses with a microfibre cloth. Additionally, keep frames away from heat. Finally, tighten screws periodically.

In summary, proper care extends the life of your accessories. Consequently, you'll save money long-term.
