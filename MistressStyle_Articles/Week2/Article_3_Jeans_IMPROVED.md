## Denim 101: Essential Jean Terminology

Before we get to recommendations, let's decode jean-speak. Understanding these terms will make shopping much easier.

**[IMAGE: Annotated jean diagram showing rise, leg opening, inseam]**

### Rise: Where Jeans Sit on Your Body

The rise determines where the waistband sits:

- **Low-Rise:** Below belly button (2-3" below)
- **Mid-Rise:** At belly button  
- **High-Rise:** Above belly button (1-3" above)
- **Ultra High-Rise:** Well above belly button (3-4"+)

### Leg Opening & Style Options

Different leg openings create different silhouettes:

- **Skinny:** Tight through entire leg, small ankle opening
- **Straight:** Same width from hip to ankle
- **Bootcut:** Slight flare from knee down
- **Flare:** More dramatic flare from knee
- **Wide-Leg:** Full, wide leg throughout
- **Boyfriend:** Relaxed, slightly baggy
- **Mom Jean:** High-waisted, tapered leg, relaxed fit

**[IMAGE: Side-by-side comparison of all jean leg styles]**

### Understanding Stretch Levels

The amount of stretch affects both comfort and appearance:

- **No Stretch:** 100% cotton, rigid denim
- **Low Stretch:** 98% cotton, 2% elastane
- **Medium Stretch:** 90-95% cotton, 5-10% stretch
- **High Stretch:** 70-85% cotton, 15-30% stretch

### Length Variations

Finally, consider the hem length:

- **Regular:** Hits at ankle bone
- **Crop/Ankle:** Hits above ankle bone
- **Cropped:** Hits at mid-calf

### Understanding Sizing: The Size-Down Debate

**[IMAGE: Infographic about sizing decisions]**

#### When to Size Down

Consider sizing down when:
- You're wearing high-stretch jeans (they'll stretch out throughout the day)
- You're between sizes (smaller usually works better)
- They feel slightly loose when brand new

#### When NOT to Size Down

Avoid sizing down when:
- They're already uncomfortable
- You can't sit comfortably
- The button is hard to close
- You're buying low-stretch or rigid denim

**Pro Tip:** If you're between sizes, go with the larger size. You can always tailor in, but letting out is difficult or impossible.

- May need frequent washing

**Best for:** Skinny jeans, comfort prioritizers

Wash every 5-7 wears (spot clean between). Furthermore, this preserves color and shape.

#### Temperature

#### Method
Turn inside out first. Then, use gentle cycle. Consequently, this protects the fabric.

#### Detergent
Use small amount only. Moreover, choose gentle detergent. Finally, avoid harsh chemicals.

#### Drying
Air dry flat or hang. Never machine dry high heat. Therefore, this prevents shrinking and maintains shape.
