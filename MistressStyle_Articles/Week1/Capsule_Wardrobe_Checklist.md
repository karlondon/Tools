## SHOPPING STRATEGY REMINDERS

**Before You Buy:**
- [ ] Check if you already own something similar
- [ ] Ensure item matches your color palette (Black, White, Denim Blue)
- [ ] Verify it fits well NOW (not "someday")
- [ ] Read online reviews
- [ ] Check care instructions (avoid hand-wash only)

**To Save Money:**
- [ ] Sign up for email lists (15-20% off first purchase)
- [ ] Download cashback apps (Rakuten, Honey, Ibotta)
- [ ] Shop during major sales (January, July, Black Friday)
- [ ] Check secondhand first (ThredUp, Poshmark, Depop)
- [ ] Stack discounts when possible

**Quality Checks:**
- [ ] Straight, secure seams and stitching
- [ ] Good fabric quality (not too thin or scratchy)
- [ ] Functioning zippers and buttons
- [ ] Comfortable fit and feel
